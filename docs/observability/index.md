# Observability & Monitoring

Welcome to the SovereignSecure Cloud Observability documentation. Deep visibility into infrastructure and application performance is critical for maintaining high availability and security.

## The Observability Stack

Our cloud utilizes a modern, open-source observability stack designed to capture metrics, logs, and traces across the entire environment:

* **Prometheus & Grafana:** The core engine for time-series metrics collection, alerting, and visual dashboarding.

## Tenant Visibility

Through the Cloud Management Platform (CMP), tenants can view resource utilization (CPU, memory, network) for their specific projects. Advanced users can also expose custom application metrics to the cloud's Prometheus endpoints or deploy isolated monitoring stacks within their own Kubernetes clusters.
