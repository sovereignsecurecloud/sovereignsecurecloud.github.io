# Compliance & Sovereignty

As a true Sovereign Cloud, our primary commitment is to protect your data from foreign jurisdiction access, unauthorized surveillance, and vendor lock-in. SovereignSecure Cloud is built to satisfy the strictest regulatory and data localization requirements.

## Data Residency and Localization

* **Onshore Data Centers:** All compute, storage, and networking infrastructure is located exclusively within our national borders. 
* **Legal Immunity:** Because our infrastructure is entirely locally owned and operated, your data is immune to foreign extraterritorial laws (such as the US CLOUD Act). No foreign entity can compel access to your data.
* **Open Source Foundation:** Our use of 100% open-source technologies (OpenStack, Ceph, Kubernetes) ensures that there are no proprietary backdoors or hidden telemetry streams phoning home to foreign tech providers.

## Security Certifications

Our infrastructure operations align with top-tier security standards, including:

* **ISO/IEC 27001:** Information Security Management.
* **SOC 2 Type II:** Security, Availability, and Confidentiality.
* **Local Government Baselines:** We comply with national cybersecurity frameworks required for hosting public sector and critical infrastructure workloads.

For official compliance artifacts, audit reports, or specific regulatory questionnaires, please contact your account manager.
