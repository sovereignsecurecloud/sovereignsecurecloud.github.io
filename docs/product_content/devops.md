## Overview
TCS DevOps as a Managed Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) empowers government and public sector agencies to adopt agile, secure, and compliant software delivery practices. Built on sovereign cloud principles, this service ensures all development, deployment, and monitoring workflows remain within national boundaries, aligning with DPDP, GDPR, and sectoral regulations.

- Agile Task Management

- Security and Scalability
- Compliant by Design

- Data Sovereignty
- Operational Efficiency
- Audit & Reporting Ready
