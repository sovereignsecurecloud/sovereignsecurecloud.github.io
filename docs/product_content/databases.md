## Overview
TCS managed Database as a Service provides a fully managed database platform. The service offering includes all the necessary components to run the DB platform, from hardware and software to continuous management services. The database platform offering covers the hardware provisioning, configuration, backup, and health check from the database users and helps organizations move away from a complex, siloed database ecosystem management to a business sensitive, agile and centralized environment.


## Benefits
- Flexibility to choose from multiple database distribution platforms 
- Multiple deployment options - Single / Multi DC solution as per the customer’s requirements
- Rapid provisioning and creation of new databases to lower cost and improve time-to-market
- Lower Cost of Ownership as basic support services are integrated
- Customized DB Solution as per the business requirements
- End to end DB managed services
- Integrated Monitoring
- Business SLAs customized as per the business requirements
