## Overview
TCS Managed Compute Services provides secure, resizable, and high-performance compute capacity in the cloud, designed to accelerate innovation and optimize workloads. Whether for web-scale applications, batch processing, or mission-critical databases, our platform offers a wide range of Virtual Machines, Serverless options, and Dedicated Hosts. Built on enterprise-grade infrastructure, TCS compute services ensure operational excellence, scalability, and cost-efficiency for every business need.
