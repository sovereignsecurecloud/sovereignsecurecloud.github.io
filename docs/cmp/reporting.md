# Chargeback & Reporting

The SovereignSecure Cloud Management Platform (CMP), powered by ManageIQ, offers robust **Chargeback and Reporting** capabilities. These features provide transparency into resource consumption and associated costs, enabling better financial governance, resource optimization, and accountability across your organization.

## What is Chargeback and Showback?

*   **Chargeback:** A mechanism to bill internal departments or projects for their actual cloud resource usage. This promotes cost awareness and encourages efficient resource utilization.
*   **Showback:** Similar to chargeback, but without actual billing. It provides departments with a detailed breakdown of their cloud consumption and what it *would* cost, fostering accountability and informed decision-making without direct financial transactions.

Both chargeback and showback are crucial for understanding where cloud spending occurs, identifying areas for optimization, and ensuring that resource usage aligns with business value.

## Accessing Reports in the CMP

To view and generate reports within the SovereignSecure Cloud Console:

1.  **Log in** to the SovereignSecure Cloud Console.
2.  Navigate to **Cloud Management (CMP) > Chargeback & Reporting**.
3.  You will typically find a dashboard summarizing key metrics, along with options to access detailed reports.

## Types of Available Reports

The CMP provides a variety of pre-configured and customizable reports to give you insights into your cloud environment:

*   **Resource Utilization Reports:**
    *   **CPU Usage:** Average, peak, and idle CPU utilization across instances or projects.
    *   **Memory Usage:** Track allocated vs. consumed memory for VMs and services.
    *   **Storage Consumption:** Monitor block storage, object storage, and snapshot usage.
    *   **Network Traffic:** Analyze inbound and outbound data transfer.
*   **Service Cost Reports:**
    *   **Service-Specific Costs:** Breakdown of costs per service item provisioned from the catalog.
    *   **Project/Department Costs:** Aggregate costs for all resources consumed by a specific project or department.
    *   **User Costs:** Track resource costs attributed to individual users.
*   **Trend Analysis Reports:**
    *   **Historical Usage:** View resource consumption trends over time (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly).
    *   **Cost Projections:** Forecast future spending based on current usage patterns.
*   **Compliance & Governance Reports:**
    *   **Quota Adherence:** Reports on whether projects are operating within their defined resource quotas.
    *   **Policy Violations:** Identify instances or services that do not comply with established policies.

## Customizing and Scheduling Reports

You can often customize reports to fit your specific needs:

*   **Filtering:** Apply filters based on project, department, tag, resource type, or time period.
*   **Grouping:** Group data by various attributes to get different perspectives (e.g., group by region, then by project).
*   **Scheduling:** Set up reports to run automatically at specified intervals (e.g., weekly, monthly) and be delivered to designated recipients.

## Exporting Report Data

Most reports can be exported in common formats for further analysis or integration with external systems:

*   CSV (Comma Separated Values)
*   PDF
*   Excel (XLSX)
