# Getting Help & Support

SovereignSecure Cloud is backed by an expert, onshore support and engineering team dedicated to keeping your critical infrastructure running smoothly.

## How to Get Help

If you experience an issue or have a technical question, you can engage our support team through the following channels:

### 1. Cloud Management Platform (CMP) Ticketing
The fastest way to get support is by raising a ticket directly within the CMP. 
Navigate to the **Support** tab in the main navigation menu. Submitting a ticket here automatically attaches your Project ID and environment context, allowing our engineers to investigate faster.

### 2. Email Support
You can email our support desk at `support@sovereignsecurecloud.com`. Please include your Organization Name, Project ID, and specific resource IDs (like Instance UUIDs) in your email.

## Ticket Severities

When raising a ticket, please select the appropriate severity level:
* **Severity 1 (Critical):** Complete loss of service or catastrophic data impact. Production environments down.
* **Severity 2 (High):** Severe performance degradation or loss of a major function, but workarounds are available.
* **Severity 3 (Normal):** General inquiries, non-critical bugs, or configuration assistance.
