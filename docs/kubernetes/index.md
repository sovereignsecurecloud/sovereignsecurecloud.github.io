# Containers & Kubernetes Overview

Welcome to the SovereignSecure Cloud Containers & Kubernetes documentation. We provide robust, secure, and sovereign environments for orchestrating your containerized workloads.

Whether you are deploying lightweight edge workloads or managing enterprise-grade scalable clusters, our Kubernetes offerings are seamlessly integrated with our underlying OpenStack Infrastructure and SDN layers.

## Core Kubernetes Offerings

Explore the following sections to learn more about our Kubernetes capabilities:

* **[Kubernetes with K3s](k3s.md):** Learn about our lightweight, highly efficient K3s distribution, ideal for rapid provisioning and resource-constrained environments.
* **[Kubernetes as a Service (KaaS)](kaas.md):** Discover our enterprise-grade KaaS offering powered by Cluster API (CAPI) for declarative, automated cluster lifecycle management.
* **[Deploying Clusters](clusters.md):** Step-by-step guides on how to deploy and manage your Kubernetes clusters through our Cloud Management Platform (CMP) or IaC tools.

## Security and Sovereignty

Our Kubernetes platforms run entirely within your dedicated Virtual Private Clouds (VPCs). This ensures that your container traffic, control planes, and persistent volumes adhere to the same strict data sovereignty and isolation policies as the rest of your cloud infrastructure.
